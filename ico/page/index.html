WHAT IS MEEYLAND ECOSYSTEM

The MeeyLand ecosystem is a Technology product chain, comprised of specialized platform applications Real Estate, with a combination of Real Estate - Technology - Finance, and pressure Applying Technology in Real Estate investment later.

The MeeyLand ecosystem is in the process of being finalized and is expected to broadcast in the near future Develop up to 25 products or more, are the applications with the most modern Technology, including the the most useful information, helping customers to save maximum effort in searching and checking information related to Real Estate, helping traders to connect directly, quickly, Cut the cost.

USER COMMUNITY

- All individual customers, business customers wishing to buy / sell / rent / transfer transfer / trade in Real Estate.

- Use a single MeeyID account to log in for all the applications of the Joint Stock Company Issued by Meey Land Group.

- Help users find the most useful information related to Real Estate such as location location, pricing, planning, legal corridors, legal status (notarization).

- In the MeeyLand ecosystem, there are special applications for Entrepreneurs large businesses such as Bank, Investor. This app makes it easy for Business owners to access potential customers.

The project is divided into 18 phases:

Building facilities, perfecting the organizational structure

Launched www.meeyland.com version 1

Launched website meeyland.com version 2

Marketing meeyland.com to the market

Start project other products like MeeyMaps, MeeyChat, MeeyID, MeeyCRM, MeeyWeb, MeeyAds, MeeyMail

Launched meeyland.com App version

Develops product projects for MeeyValue, MeeyNews, MeeyReport, MeeyPay, Meey3D

Development of MeeyEdu, MeeyWallet, MeeyReport, MeeyChain products
